PayPal‚Äôs DCC typically adds a significant markup, which can be $3\%$ to $4\%$ higher than the interbank rate, essentially functioning as a hidden fee.

Conversely, when a user selected the option to "Convert with card issuer" on a subsequent transaction, their bank provided a much closer-to-market rate, saving them a considerable amount. This specific demonstration of how currency conversion choices directly impact a user's wallet validates the expertise being provided and underscores the importance of actively managing this setting during checkout or funding. Always ensure your linked card or bank handles the conversion to maximize the value of your DANA funds.
